How I work

If it could've been done by anyone, it isn't done yet.

01

The obvious version comes first

There's always a direction that would be easy to approve. I build it to understand what I'm trying to push past. The moment it could have come from anyone is the moment I know where to go next.

02

AI cuts the distance between vision and output

I use AI to compress the gap between having an idea and having something real to react to. When the right tool doesn't exist, I build it. Custom scripts, utilities, systems specific to how the project needs to work.

03

It becomes mine when I stop checking

At some point in most projects I notice I've stopped looking at references. Not intentionally. I just realize I haven't checked what others are doing in a while. That's usually when the work starts to feel like it's going somewhere.
