How I work

From vague idea to structured outcome.

The work starts by making the unclear legible: what needs to be understood, who needs it, and what kind of form can carry it without losing texture.

01

Clarify the problem

What needs to become understandable? Who needs it? What is confusing, hidden, or emotionally loaded?

02

Create the system

Define the visual language, information hierarchy, interaction logic, and repeatable rules.

03

Make it tangible

Move into screens, identity assets, prototypes, spatial ideas, content, or frontend implementation.

04

Reflect and improve

Document what changed in the project and which principle can be reused in the next one.
