How I work

From vague idea to structured outcome.

This page should make you look more senior: it explains your working method before people even open a project.

01

Clarify the problem

What needs to become understandable? Who needs it? What is confusing, hidden, or emotionally loaded?

02

Create the system

Define the visual language, information hierarchy, interaction logic, and repeatable rules.

03

Make it tangible

Move into screens, identity assets, prototypes, spatial ideas, content, or frontend implementation.

04

Reflect and improve

Document what the project taught, what changed in your process, and what principle you can reuse.
